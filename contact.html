Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

Are you currently hiring?

We're actively building our team as we establish operations in Tokyo. While we're not openly advertising positions yet, we welcome inquiries from talented robotics engineers, AI specialists, and business development professionals. Send your resume to quielo@hanzotechnologies.com.

Do you accept investment inquiries?

Yes! We're actively seeking strategic investors and venture capital partners who share our vision for robotics innovation. Please contact us at quielo@hanzotechnologies.com for investment opportunities and pitch deck materials.

Can I schedule a product demonstration?

Our products are currently in development. We'll be scheduling demonstrations once prototypes are available. If you're interested in early access or pilot programs, please reach out to outreach@hanzotechnologies.com.

How can research institutions partner with you?

We're actively seeking research partnerships with institutions like RIKEN, AIST, and leading universities. For collaboration opportunities, please contact outreach@hanzotechnologies.com with details about your research focus and potential synergies.

Do you have any products available for purchase?

Our products are currently in development and not yet commercially available. We're following a phased development strategy with our super app launching first, followed by our robotics platforms. Join our contact list to be notified when products become available.

What languages do you support?

Our primary business languages are English and Japanese. Our website offers 12 language options to serve our global audience. Our robots will support voice interaction in Japanese and English, with additional languages planned for future releases.
